Unlocking the Genome

Join the Ren Lab at UNC Charlotte for an immersive journey into the microscopic world that dictates health and disease.

Chromatin Biology

Discover how DNA is packaged inside the nucleus. Understand the "beads on a string" structure and how chromatin accessibility controls gene expression.

Cancer Epigenetics

Explore how "glitches" in the epigenetic code can lead to cancer. We'll discuss how modern research targets these mechanisms for therapy.

In Silico CRISPR

Learn to use safe, computer-based simulation tools to design CRISPR experiments. No wet-lab risks—just pure data and genomic design.
